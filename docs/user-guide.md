## Getting Started

1. Open the app in your browser.
2. Fill out each section of your project plan using the guided forms.
3. Use the "Suggest with AI" button for help or inspiration in any section.

---

## Using the App

- Navigate through each project plan section (Overview, Objectives, Scope, etc.).
- Each section includes a description and a form field for your input.
- Progress is tracked so you can see what’s left to complete.

---

## AI Suggestions

- Click the "Suggest with AI" button in any section.
- Your current text and a pre-configured prompt will be copied to your clipboard.
- Choose your preferred AI service (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini) and paste the prompt for suggestions.
- Copy the improved text back into the app.

---

## Exporting Your Plan

- When your plan is complete, click the "Export" button.
- Choose your preferred format: PDF, Word, Markdown, or Excel.
- The file will be generated and downloaded to your device.

---

## Saving and Loading

- Use the "Save" button to store your plan in your browser or download it as a file.
- Use the "Load" button to import a previously saved plan.

---

## Troubleshooting

- **The app won’t load:** Make sure you’re using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).
- **Export isn’t working:** Check that pop-ups and downloads are allowed in your browser.
- **AI suggestions aren’t helpful:** Try rephrasing your input or providing more context.

## FAQ

**Q: Is my data stored online?**  
A: No, all data is stored locally in your browser or on your device unless you choose to use a cloud integration (future feature).

**Q: Can I collaborate with others?**  
A: Collaboration features are planned for a future release.

**Q: How do I report a bug or request a feature?**  
A: Please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/saint1415/project-plan-app/issues).
